═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: "mvn: The term 'mvn' is not recognized"
A: Maven not installed. Download and install from:
   https://maven.apache.org/download.cgi

Q: Port 8080 already in use
A: Edit src/main/resources/application.properties
   Change: server.port=9090 (or any free port)

Q: Words not loading
A: Ensure words.txt exists in project root
   Run: mvn clean spring-boot:run

Q: Compilation errors
A: Try: mvn clean compile
   Then: mvn spring-boot:run
